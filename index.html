Enamel Collection

Enamel jewellery is a vibrant form of adornment that incorporates colored glass fused to metal, typically gold or silver. This technique dates back thousands of years and allows for intricate designs and patterns. Enamel can be applied in various styles, including cloisonné, champlevé, and painted enamel. The result is a stunning contrast between the shiny metal and the colorful enamel, making each piece unique. Enamel jewellery can range from delicate earrings and pendants to bold statement pieces. Its durability and resistance to tarnishing make it a popular choice, while its artistic appeal ensures it remains a favored option for collectors and fashion enthusiasts alike,making their creations not only beautiful but also environmentally conscious, resonating with today’s values.

Contemporary Collection

Contemporary jewellery reflects modern design trends, emphasizing creativity, innovation, and individuality. It often combines unconventional materials like acrylic, wood, and metal, challenging traditional aesthetics. Artists in this field experiment with forms and techniques, resulting in bold, statement pieces that express personal style. Contemporary jewellery can range from minimalist designs to intricate, sculptural works, often incorporating elements of art and fashion. This style appeals to those seeking unique accessories that stand out. Additionally, many contemporary jewellery designers focus on sustainability, using recycled materials and ethical practices, making their creations not only beautiful but also environmentally conscious, resonating with today’s values.

Crystal Collection

Crystal jewellery is crafted using natural or synthetic crystals, renowned for their beauty and metaphysical properties.These pieces often feature gemstones like quartz, amethyst, and Swarovski crystals, each offering unique colors and clarity.
